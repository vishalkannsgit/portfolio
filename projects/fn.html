The Fake News Classifier achieves a high F1-score, balancing precision and recall effectively. It can handle large-scale diverse datasets and provide reliable predictions that can be integrated into real-world news platforms.

üìÇ Dataset

This project uses the WELFake Dataset containing 72,134 news articles (35,028 real and 37,106 fake). It merges data from Kaggle, McIntire, Reuters, and BuzzFeed Political to ensure robustness and prevent overfitting.
